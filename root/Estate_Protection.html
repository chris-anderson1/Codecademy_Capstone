Estate Protection

Preserving your legacy and ensuring the smooth transfer of your wealth is a vital part of your financial plan. Iron Range Wealth Management offers estate protection services to help you safeguard your assets and fulfill your wishes for your estate. Our experienced advisors provide comprehensive strategies tailored to your needs.

Services Offered:


  • Estate Planning Consultation: Provide expert advice on creating a comprehensive estate plan.

  • Trust Services: Establish and manage trusts to protect and transfer your assets.

  • Will Preparation: Assist in drafting wills to ensure your wishes are clearly documented.

  • Wealth Transfer Strategies: Develop strategies for the efficient transfer of wealth to your heirs.

  • Beneficiary Designations: Review and update beneficiary designations on your accounts.
